I am a Research Engineer with [ROAHM Lab](https://www.roahmlab.com/), supervised by Professor Ram Vasudevan in the Department of Robotics at the University of Michigan.
My work there focuses on safe robot planning with perception and the development of research tools for robotics.

I hold a Masters in Robotics from the University of Michigan and a Bachelors in Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.

I am particularly interested in the emerging technologies that are likely to define our lives in the future.
I believe there exists some intersection between robotics, deep learning, and VR/AR.
This belief informs my greater professional interests.
